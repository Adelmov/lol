Learn to Play Guitar Fast, Simple, and Easy

Wanna Start Playing?

All you need to start playing is passion and a guitar, and you'll pick the rest up as you go. On the topic of picks, if you want to play songs on guitar, you'll most likely need a guitar pick, a guitar strap to play comfortably, and a tuner.

Planning on playing live?

If you want to play an electric or electric acoustic guitar, get ready to jam! To start off, you'll needn your guitar, an amp, and a cable. As you grow as a guitarist you can start to get into effects and pedals to try cool sounds.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to learn guitar?

Besides your guitar and other accessories such as picks, straps, tuners, and spare strings, you'll need a cirriculum or set of books or any guitar tabs you would like to learn. Using a metronome is also very necessary to keep a steady tempo.

Does my guitar need to be tuned?

Your guitar must alway be properly tuned so all the chords and melodies sound good as you play. A vast variety of tuners can easily clip onto your guitar making it easy to tune.
